projectIdea:
The bussiness idea is about the "amateur football championships"
We will have 3 entities :
1.User - who can create ,update,delete his/her championships  and teams
2.Championship- Every championship has many teams and a trophy and a FK to the user who created it and also the total 
number of matches that a team can play
3.Team - a team has a name,the number of matches played count and the points as well as a Fk to the championship
projectDescription:
 A user can manage football championships for amateur players and can keep track of the scores regarding the matches 
that they played.
